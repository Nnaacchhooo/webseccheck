High X-Frame-Options Header Missing FAIL ‚Ä∫

Description

The X-Frame-Options header is not present. This header prevents your site from being embedded in iframes on malicious websites. While your CSP includes frame-ancestors for Tienda Nube domains, the legacy X-Frame-Options header provides defense-in-depth for older browsers.

Impact

  • Clickjacking attacks: Attackers overlay invisible iframes of your store, tricking customers into clicking buttons they can't see
  • Customers could unknowingly add items to cart, proceed to checkout, or submit forms
  • Particularly dangerous for e-commerce with authenticated checkout flows
  • May affect PCI DSS compliance requirements for protecting cardholder data entry

E-Commerce Risk

HIGH ‚Äî Online stores are prime targets for clickjacking as attackers can trick users into initiating purchases or revealing payment details.

Remediation ‚Äî Cloudflare Dashboard

Go to Rules ‚Üí Transform Rules ‚Üí Modify Response Header and add:

Header: X-Frame-Options
Value: SAMEORIGIN

Use SAMEORIGIN since Tienda Nube may need to embed your store in its admin iframes. If not needed, use DENY for maximum protection.

Tienda Nube Platform Note

This header may need to be configured at the Tienda Nube (Nuvemshop) platform level. Contact Tienda Nube support to request enabling X-Frame-Options, or use Cloudflare Transform Rules to add it at the CDN edge.

High DMARC Record Missing FAIL ‚Ä∫

Description

No DMARC (Domain-based Message Authentication, Reporting & Conformance) record found at _dmarc.divinohome.com. DMARC ties SPF and DKIM together and tells receiving servers what to do with unauthenticated email.

Impact

  • Attackers can send phishing emails that appear to come from @divinohome.com
  • Critical for e-commerce: Fake order confirmations, shipping updates, or payment requests could trick your customers
  • Damages domain reputation and email deliverability
  • Fails Google/Yahoo 2024 sender requirements ‚Äî your emails may land in spam
  • No visibility into who is sending email on behalf of your domain

Remediation ‚Äî DNS Record

Add a TXT record in your DNS provider (Cloudflare or domain registrar):

Name: _dmarc
Type: TXT
Value: v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@divinohome.com; ruf=mailto:dmarc@divinohome.com; fo=1;

Start with p=none (monitor mode), review reports for 4-6 weeks, then escalate to p=quarantine and eventually p=reject.

Progressive Enforcement

  • Week 1-4: p=none ‚Äî collect reports, identify legitimate senders
  • Week 5-8: p=quarantine; pct=25 ‚Äî quarantine 25%
  • Week 9+: p=reject ‚Äî full enforcement

üí° Consider a free DMARC monitoring service like Postmark DMARC to easily parse reports.

Medium Referrer-Policy Header Missing FAIL ‚Ä∫

Description

The Referrer-Policy header is not set. Without it, the browser's default behavior sends the full URL (including query parameters) to third-party sites when users click external links or when third-party resources are loaded.

Impact

  • Customer browsing patterns and product interests leaked to third-party analytics and ad networks
  • Internal URL structure (admin panels, order IDs, session tokens in URLs) exposed to external services
  • For e-commerce: customer order tracking URLs and account pages may leak sensitive identifiers

Remediation ‚Äî Cloudflare

Rules ‚Üí Transform Rules ‚Üí Modify Response Header:

Header: Referrer-Policy
Value: strict-origin-when-cross-origin

This sends only the origin (domain) to cross-origin requests while keeping the full URL for same-origin navigation ‚Äî the best balance of privacy and functionality.

Tienda Nube Note

This header can be added via Cloudflare Transform Rules without any changes to the Tienda Nube platform configuration.

Medium Permissions-Policy Header Missing FAIL ‚Ä∫

Description

The Permissions-Policy (formerly Feature-Policy) header is not set. This header controls which browser features and APIs can be used on your site and by embedded third-party content.

Impact

  • Third-party scripts (analytics, chat widgets, ads) could access camera, microphone, geolocation without restriction
  • Embedded content from payment providers or social widgets may request unnecessary permissions
  • No control over which browser APIs third-party code can use

Remediation ‚Äî Cloudflare

Rules ‚Üí Transform Rules ‚Üí Modify Response Header:

Header: Permissions-Policy
Value: camera=(), microphone=(), geolocation=(), interest-cohort=()

This disables camera, microphone, geolocation, and FLoC/Topics API for all contexts. An e-commerce store typically doesn't need any of these ‚Äî safe to disable entirely.

Low DKIM Not Found for Common Selectors WARN ‚Ä∫

Description

No DKIM records were found for common selectors (default, google, selector1, selector2, k1, mail, dkim). DKIM cryptographically signs outgoing emails to prove they haven't been tampered with. Tienda Nube or your email provider may use a custom selector not checked in this scan.

Impact

  • If DKIM is truly missing, emails may fail authentication at strict receivers
  • Required for full DMARC enforcement
  • Reduced email deliverability ‚Äî order confirmations and shipping notifications may land in spam

Remediation

Check with your email provider (Tienda Nube's built-in email, or any third-party service you use) for their specific DKIM selector and DNS records. Common steps:

  • Check Tienda Nube admin panel for email authentication settings
  • If using a transactional email service (Mailgun, SendGrid, etc.), follow their DKIM setup guide
  • Add the provided CNAME/TXT records to your DNS

Verification

Send a test email to mail-tester.com to check if DKIM is actually signing your outgoing emails with a custom selector.
